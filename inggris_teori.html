Materi Teori Bahasa Inggris

Salam dan Sapaan

Pelajari berbagai salam dan sapaan dalam bahasa Inggris:
- Hello / Hi (Halo)
- Good morning / Good afternoon / Good evening (Selamat pagi/siang/malam)
- How are you? / I'm fine, thank you. (Apa kabar? / Baik, terima kasih)
- Goodbye / See you / Take care (Selamat tinggal / Sampai jumpa / Jaga diri)

The Eight Parts of Speech

Pelajari 8 jenis kata dalam bahasa Inggris beserta contohnya:
1. Noun (Kata benda): cat, school, book
2. Pronoun (Kata ganti): he, she, it
3. Verb (Kata kerja): eat, run, study
4. Adjective (Kata sifat): beautiful, big, fast
5. Adverb (Kata keterangan): quickly, very, well
6. Preposition (Kata depan): in, on, at
7. Conjunction (Kata penghubung): and, but, because
8. Interjection (Seru): wow!, oh!, hey!

Contoh Dialog Salam

Dialog sederhana:
A: Hello! How are you?
B: Hi! I'm fine, thank you. And you?
A: I'm good too. See you later!
B: Goodbye!

Latihan Percakapan

Latihlah sapaan dan perkenalan:
- Introduce yourself: My name is Sarah. What's your name?
- Menanyakan kabar: How are you today?
- Mengucapkan selamat tinggal: See you tomorrow / Take care!
